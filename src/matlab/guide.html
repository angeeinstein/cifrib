Getting Started Guide

Step 1: Add the Bar

Input the bar length in the “Length” field under the Bar Setup panel. Click “Add” to implement the bar in the workspace.

Step 2: Define Bar Profile

Select one of four bar profiles under the “Bar Profile” tab. This helps in calculating the safety factor against yielding and fracture.

Step 3: Define Supports

Select the type of support (e.g., clamped, roller, or hinge) from the Support panel. Specify the position and click “Add.”

Step 4: Add Forces and Moments

Specify the force magnitude, angle, and position in the “Add Loads” panel. Click “Add” to apply.

Step 5: Add Distributed Line Loads

Select linear or quadratic load, define start/end positions, height, and pitch, then click “Add.”

Step 6: Modify or Adjust Inputs

Use Undo/Redo buttons in the top menu. (Coming soon: Click elements to edit properties.)

Step 7: Verify and Calculate

Check the status indicator. Green means calculations can proceed. Click “Calculate” to view results.
